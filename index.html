FAQ

Common questions, before applying.

How is the cohort different from the free masterclass and the digital course?

The masterclass is the methodology pre-frame. The Get the Girlies Richer digital course is the self-paced install. Be Rich™ Branding Mastery is the cohort, where the methodology gets built alongside a group of female founders running the same framework with live coaching, weekly office hours, and direct access to Blair.

Mid tier or flagship cohort, which one is for me?

Mid tier (Get the Girlies Richer digital course) if you're past the masterclass and want to install the framework on your own time. Flagship (Be Rich™ Branding Mastery cohort) if you want the live cohort, the weekly office hours, the direct access, and the accountability. The diagnostic on this page routes the conversation in 90 seconds.

How fast will I see results?

Students inside Be Rich™ Branding Mastery have collectively earned $3M in 16 months. Individual timelines vary by starting stage. Most founders inside the cohort install the brand architecture within the first 30 days and report first paid product launches inside the first 60.

What's the time commitment?

The masterclass is 2 hours. The Get the Girlies Richer digital course is self-paced. Be Rich™ Branding Mastery is a cohort with live group calls and weekly office hours. The VIP / 1:1 track is bespoke.

How does the investment work across the tiers?

The masterclass is free. The digital course is the publicly-available mid-tier. Be Rich™ Branding Mastery cohort and the VIP / 1:1 track are application-only because the fit conversation matters more than the figure at those levels. Stage-specific and confirmed at intake.

Where is Blair based?

Blair runs GTGR4L LLC remote-first. The community on Instagram and TikTok crosses North America, the UK, and Europe. Time zone flexibility built into the cohort cadence.

Why a diagnostic instead of a single front door?

Because the stage you're in matters more than the program name. A founder pre-brand and a founder already running a digital product business at six figures do not need the same install. The diagnostic routes the conversation to the right tier so the discovery starts in the right place.

What if I'm not sure which tier?

Take the diagnostic. If it's still unclear, apply for the closest tier and the fit conversation routes you on intake. No setter calls, no back-and-forth, just a clear next step.
